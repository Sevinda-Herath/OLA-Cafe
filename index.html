About Us

A Cozy Café
by the Ocean

Nestled along the scenic route to Hummanaya, our oceanfront café in Nakulugamuwa offers great food, refreshing drinks, and stunning sea views. Whether you're here for a quick stop or a relaxing stay, our cozy rooms and warm hospitality make it the perfect coastal getaway.
